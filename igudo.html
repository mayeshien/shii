IGUDO RECIPE

Stewed Pork,liver, and kidney with green peas and boil pepper. This popular Filipino recipe is a must try

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 lbs. pork tanderloin sliced into 2 inch strips
  • 1/2 lb. pork liver sliced into 2 inch strips
  • 1/2 lb. pork kidney
  • 1 knorr pork cube
  • 1 red pepper cut into strips
  • 1/2 green peas
  • 1 carrot julienne
  • 3 laurel leaves
  • 1 onion minced
  • 5 gloves garlic minced
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauace
  • 5 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 1/4 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the kidney with 4 thumbs of ginger for at least 30 minutes. Let it cool down and slice into 2 inch strips. Set aside.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil in a pan. Sayte the pork liver for 2 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Pour the remaining oil in the pan. Once hot, saute garlic and onion until the latter soltens.
  4. Add the pork tenderloin. cooked for 1 minute.
  5. Add the pork tanderloin. Saute until the outer layer turns light brown.
  6. pour the soy sauce and vinegar into the pan. Let the liquid boil. stir.
  7. Add the laurel leaves and pour water. Let boil. Reduce the heat between low to medium setting. Continue cooking for 40 minutes.
  8. Add Knorr Pork Cube. Cook for 8 minutes.
  9. Add the liver back into the pan. Stir and continue cooking for 3 minutes
  10. Put the bell pepper, carrot, and green peas
  11. Season with salt and ground black pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve hot with rice
  12. Share and enjoy
